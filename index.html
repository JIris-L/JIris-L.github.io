About
关于我

I am interested in how people read, write, and create outside of formal spaces—online, in fandoms, in student societies—and how these practices can be taken seriously.

我好奇的是：人们在正式课堂之外如何阅读、写作与创作——在网络上、 在同人圈、在社团与日常小聚中——以及这些实践如何被认真对待。

This website is a small archive to hold these pieces together: the writing, the projects, and the worlds that are still in progress.

这个网站是一个小小的档案馆，用来安放这些仍在进行中的片段： 文本、项目，以及尚未完结的世界。
