Moonlit Crossings · 月迷津渡

本升硕 / 文学爱好者 / 数字文化观察者

Undergraduate-to-Master’s student navigating between literature, digital culture, and community spaces. A Level 6 Bilibili user, an ordinary follower of internet celebrities, a devoted BJD collector, a long-time savior of the Forgotten Realms, and a Warrior of Light in another world.

本升硕，徘徊在文学、数字文化与社群空间之间。B 站六级用户、网红关注者、 BJD 娃娘、费伦大陆救世主，也是另一个世界的光之战士。

I am interested in how people read, write, and create outside formal spaces—on the internet, in fandoms, through student societies—and in how these practices might be taken seriously.

好奇人们在课堂之外如何阅读、写作与创作——在网络、在同人圈、在社团与 日常聚会中——以及这些实践如何被认真对待。
